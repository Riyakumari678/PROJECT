BIHAR ENGINEEERING UNIVERSITY

Bihar Engineering University(BEU),Patna is a state-run public university established by government of Bihar to oversee and coordinate engineering education across the state.The university aims to ensure uniform academic standards,promote research and innovation,and facilitate quality technology.

GAYA COLLAGE OF ENGINEERING

Gaya Collage of Engineering(GCE),located in Gaya,Bihar,is a state-owned engineering collage.The collage is founded by the Government of Bihar and managed by the Department of Science and Technology,Bihar.GCE Gaya is affilated with Aryabhatta Knowledge University.
